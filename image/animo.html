About
Its an all female guides and Driver run company based in Kigali, Rwanda, that aims at giving equal opportunity to Rwanda Women and Girls in the field of Tourism and travel related jobs. to give tourists the good information they need based on what they are interested in. the female drivers/guides would also share how happily they feel doing this job. They take it as an opportunity to achieve their vision goals through Judith Safaris. Most of the women in Rwanda have the sad stories on their last past years of being accepted and appreciated in the society, back then they were recognized as the home keeping and taking care of families. But since the 20th century every woman tries to see the light of the vision through their country.
